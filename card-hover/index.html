Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is a shonen manga by Masashi Kishimoto that follows the life of the noisy and restless teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki, who dreams of achieving universal recognition and becoming Hokage, the head of his village and the strongest ninja.

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, nicknamed "Straw Hat", is the main character of the manga and anime One Piece: Snatch. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He ate the rubber-rubber Devil Fruit, which made him a "rubber man", and as a result, his body gained the ability to stretch.
